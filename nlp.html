Founder

joemonaghan

Executive Director

Joe’s work in the pro-life movement started as a child. Names such as Randall Terry and Joan Andrews were part of the dinner table conversation. His father helped found one of the nation's leading free-speech law firms, and Joe often accompanied him in his work.

One of Joe's fondest memories is of Big Al Swarthout who received a misdemeanor charge for driving an amphibious army transport through a California abortion clinic.

After graduating from college, Joe worked on Capitol Hill for a Congressman & Senator before transitioning to FIG (Financial Institutions Group) where he was the founding organizer of a nationally charted bank and helped structure reinsurance transactions to benefit hospitals and associations. Joe holds a Bachelors degree in Political Science from The Thomas More College of Liberal Arts, where he was the first alumnus to join the Board of Directors, and an MBA from the WP Carey School of Business at Arizona State University.
