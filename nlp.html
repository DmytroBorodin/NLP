Executive Director

Joe grew up in the pro-life movement, often sidewalk counseling with his friends, and his father is a free-speech attorney who represented pro-life activists. After graduating from college, Joe worked on Capitol Hill for a Congressman & Senator before transitioning to FIG (Financial Institutions Group) where he was the founding organizer of a nationally charted bank and helped structure reinsurance transactions to benefit hospitals and NGOs.

Joe holds a Bachelors degree in Political Science from The Thomas More College of Liberal Arts (he was the first alumnus to join the Board of Directors) and an MBA from the WP Carey School of Business at Arizona State University.
